Lasanga

Description

Lasagna a food that everyone love. Despite it's complex looks, Lasanga is quite easy to make and doesnt require all that much effort or grocerires. In this recipe we will show you how to make a perfect lasagna at home. So sit back relax and Enjoy !!!

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Tomato Sauce
  • 4 Pasta Sheet
  • 3 Sausage
  • 100g of Grated Cheese
  • Some basils (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the sausages in a pan
  2. In a baking pan put a parchment paper
  3. Put one sheet of pasta, follow by some tomato sauce then add sausage and cheese
  4. Repeat the process 3 times for 3 layers
  5. Put some tomato sauce and chesse on top
  6. Bake the Lasanga at 250C for 20 minute
  7. Garnish with some basil on a plate
