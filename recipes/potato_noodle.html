Potato Noodle

Description

Nothing is better than a hot potato noodle soup on a rainy night at Stoke.

Ingredients

  • One packet of Nongshim Potato Noodle Soup Packet
  • One egg

Steps

  1. Prepare the noodle as per the packet instructions
  2. Boil an egg for 2 min and then serve it with the noodle soup
