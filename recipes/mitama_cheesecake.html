Mitama Chessecake

Description

The following recipe is a reimagination of cheesecake made by the character Mitama from magia record animation seriees.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of supermarket cheesecake
  • Ketchup
  • Sprinkles
  • Japanese Sour plum

Steps

  1. Place the cheesecake on a plate
  2. Pour Ketchup in a zig zag pattern onto the cheesecake
  3. Shower some Sprinkles on the cheesecake
  4. Put the sour plum on top and enjoy
